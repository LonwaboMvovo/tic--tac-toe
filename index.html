Rules

  1. Players take turns placing their marks on empty squares.
  2. Players alternate who starts every new round.
  3. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across or diagonally) wins!
  4. If all 9 squares get full, the game is over. If neither player has 3 marks in a row, it's a draw.
  5. Player names must be 13 or less characters.
